Aizhai bridge

The Aizhai Bridge is a suspension bridge on the G65 Baotou–Maoming Expressway near Jishou, Hunan, China. The bridge was built as part of ... Construction end: December 2011

History

Construction on the Aizhai Bridge began in October 2007 and was completed ahead of schedule by the end of 2011. It was formally opened to traffic in March 2012. The completion of the bridge shortens the journey time between Chongqing and Changsha in this very mountainous region from several days to just eight hours.
